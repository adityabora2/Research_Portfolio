AI-driven Adaptive Noise Cancellation system

I am developing a Convolutional Recurrent Neural Network (CRNN) specifically trained to distinguish the complex sound patterns of snoring from other essential noises like speech or alarms in real-time.

The entire deep learning model is being engineered to run efficiently on a Raspberry Pi 4, integrating with Digital Signal Processing (DSP) techniques to create a smart, stand-alone noise cancellation device.

The project is focused on achieving high-performance benchmarks, targeting a 50-60% reduction in specific noises with a total system latency of under 200 milliseconds for a seamless user experience.
