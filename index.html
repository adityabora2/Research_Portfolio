Hello!

I am a final-year Computer Engineering student at Thapar Institute of Engineering an Technology, driven by a profound curiosity for using machine learning to reshape our interaction with the world. This curiosity is the central thread connecting my academic projects, research, and professional experience. I am currently engineering an AI-driven Adaptive Noise Cancellation (ANC) system by leveraging deep learning framework for intelligent noise suppression and training CRNN for distinguishing snoring sounds from other non-critical sounds like speech.

My passion for research was cultivated during my tenure as a Research Intern at the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), where I was advised by Dr. Girish Mishra ('F' Scientist). My work consisted of conducting research at the intersection of deep learning and cryptanalysis (Q-What is the impact of subtle S-Box modifications on the statistical properties of ciphertexts in the PRESENT Cipher). Prior to this, I worked at Newgen Software, as a Backend Developer Intern where I was guided by Mohnish Chopra. As a part of the 'Software Engineer Enablement Program,' I gained hands-on experience in microservices development and deploying a RESTful Spring Boot application as a proof-of-concept (PoC). I graduated from Tagore International School for the class of 2022, having successfully completed the All India Senior School Certificate Examination (CBSE).

My earliest form of education started over a decade ago at the Delhi School of Music, where I began learning guitar and was taught by Vivek Sharma. That passion has grown with me, and now I'm the Lead Guitarist for my college's band. It's a journey that has taken me from a classroom to performing on stage for over a thousand people at campus events.
